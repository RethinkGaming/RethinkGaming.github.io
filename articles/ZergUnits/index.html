Starcraft II: Zerg Units

Zerg Units

Drone

Pros

  • 5 extra hp
  • HP regen

Cons

  • Expended to make buildings

Overlord

Pros

  • Mobile Scout

Cons

  • Slow (until upgrade)

Overseer

Queen

Pros

  • Good anti-air capabilities
  • Great support abilities
  • Resource efficient, supply efficient

Cons

  • Extremely slow off creep
  • Poor ground DPS

Zergling

Pros

  • Fastest unit in game (on creep)
  • Great DPS with adrenal glands
  • Incredibly cheap

Cons

  • Fragile

Baneling

Pros

  • Great AoE damage to light armor

Cons

  • Expensive
  • Self-destructive
  • Slow off creep

Roach

Pros

  • Cheap
  • Lots of HP

Cons

  • Supply expensive

Hydralisk

Pros

  • High DPS

Cons

  • Expensive
  • Fragile

Mutalisk

Pros

  • Fast and agile
  • Gets better the more you have

Cons

  • Expensive
  • Fragile

Infestor

Pros

  • Decent offensive abilities

Cons

Swarm Host

Pros

  • Makes free units

Cons

  • Vulnerable

Ultralisk

Pros

  • Tanky
  • Great DPS

Cons

  • Expensive

Brood Lord

  • Makes free units
  • Long range
